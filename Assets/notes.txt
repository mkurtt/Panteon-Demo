Gameplay takes about 40 seconds

Move with WASD or arrows
Jump with SPACE
Cheat Leap with X

Brush also moves with WASD

You can switch camera mods by enabling or disabling isReal3rdPerson variable of CameraController.cs on Main Camera



Obstacles
Moving Obstacle
Moving Obstacle follows the paths assigned to its list, You can RESET MovingObstacle.cs on the child MovingObstacle to assign the sibling paths in order or put them as you like to create a path.

Rotator and Stick
All you need to do is to duplicate wished amount of child sticks and reset the Rotator.cs on Rotator. Velocity default is set to 150
